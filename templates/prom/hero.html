Requirements
You can get a hero to aid your {$uera.empire} any time you like. However, you can have only one of each type of hero, Warrior, Peaceful, and Special. Also, getting a hero destroyes about 40-50% of all your property, including troops and land. That said, Heroes are well worth the cost of securing them. They do not take your property, not belonging to you, and they bestow amazing powers and abilities on your {$uera.empire}. You can't get a hero unless you have demonstrated some prowess (see below), and you can't get a hero until you have used your first 1000 turns.

Hero Combinations:
If you are strong on attacking, {$war1}-{$peace1}-{$special1} is a good choice.
If you concentrate on food and wealth, {$war2}-{$peace1}/{$peace3}-{$special2} is worth it, depending on whether you are interested in food or wealth.
If you really like {$uera.wizards}, we would suggest {$war3}-{$peace2}-{$special3}.
Of course, mix and match according to your taste - that is the point!
